Our Story

Don Connects wasn't born in a boardroom. It was born from the painful lessons of failure and the powerful hope for a better future.

I know the feeling of working hard for someone else's dream, only to be fired and replaced the next day, with the system moving on without a second thought for me or my family. After that, everyone told me to just find another "good job." But I knew I couldn't go back to that trapâ€”the routine, the feeling that my own potential was being wasted.

Instead, I poured everything I had into a poultry business. It was my dream. But I learned a hard lesson that many entrepreneurs in Namibia face: a good idea isn't enough when you're on your own. I ran out of capital. I had no help with sales, no one to assist with the daily grind of feeding and cleaning. I was the only one who believed in the vision, and because I was alone, the dream failed.

Those experiences taught me a fundamental truth: many of us are trapped. We are trapped by a lack of capital, by unemployment, by the belief that we have to build our dreams all by ourselves. We are programmed by a system that tells us the biggest dream we can have is to get a "good job" working for someone else.

Don Connects is my answer to that broken system.

This is not just another app. This is my promise to every entrepreneur who has felt alone, to every skilled person who has struggled to find work, and to every parent who wants a better future for their child. It is a community built on one simple, powerful idea: you are not alone anymore.

Frequently Asked Questions

Don Connects is a future mobile app that will act as a trusted marketplace for all Namibians. It is a platform where you can find verified, reliable local people for any task you need done (like plumbing, cleaning, or graphic design). It is also a place where anyone with a skill can offer their services, find work, build a reputation, and get paid safely through our secure payment system. Our goal is to connect the community and create economic opportunities for everyone.
