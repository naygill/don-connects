Our Mission

Don Connects was born from a simple idea: to unlock the vast potential of the African workforce. We saw a disconnect between talented, hardworking individuals seeking opportunities and the people and businesses who needed their skills. Our mission is to build the bridge. We are more than just an app; we are a community built on trust, a platform for growth, and a new way for Namibians and Africans to achieve economic empowerment.

Welcome to Don Connects. These terms outline the rules for using our platform. By using our services, you agree to these terms.

1. The Platform

Don Connects is a platform that connects users seeking to provide services ("Helpers") with users seeking to obtain services ("Requesters"). We are not an employer but a facilitator of these connections.

2. User Accounts & Verification

All users must register and complete our Know Your Customer (KYC) verification process. You agree to provide accurate information and are responsible for your account's security.

3. Payments

Requesters pay the agreed job amount plus a platform fee. Payments are held in escrow by a licensed third-party payment provider and released to the Helper upon successful job completion, minus a 10% platform commission.

4. User Conduct

You agree to act professionally and respectfully. Fraud, harassment, or any illegal activity is strictly prohibited and will result in account termination and potential blacklisting.

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide during registration, including your name, identity documents, proof of residence, and contact details. We also collect data on jobs posted and completed.

Your information is used to verify your identity (KYC), connect you with other users, process payments, and ensure the security of our platform. We may use anonymized data for analytics.
